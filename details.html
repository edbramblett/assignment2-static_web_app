Spider-Man

Universe: Marvel Comics

First Appearance: Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962)

Abilities: Superhuman strength, agility, wall-crawling, spider-sense, web-shooters

Backstory: After being bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gained incredible powers. Following the death of his Uncle Ben, he learned that “with great power comes great responsibility” and dedicated his life to protecting others.
